PRESIDENT'S MESSAGE

Dear Members,

On behalf of IASO, I extend a warm welcome to each one of you to NATCON -IASO 2024 in the most happening city in India “Bengaluru” .

IASO’s rich history of excellence in cancer surgery is a testament to our visionary leadership, commitment to knowledge-sharing, and dedication to education and research.

As we collectively face the emerging global challenge of cancer, we understand the critical role surgery plays as a highly effective and widely available intervention. The constant evolution of technology and knowledge demands that we, as surgical professionals, stay at the forefront of learning, pushing the boundaries of what is possible. Our duty remains steadfast - to ensure that the highest standard of care is not just provided but is accessible and affordable to all, regardless of socio-economic background.

As we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of cancer, staying informed and engaged is crucial. IASO is committed to serving as a hub for collaboration and networking among our members, various oncology societies, and international scientific organizations through NATCON.

Through these collaborative efforts we will continue to shape the future of surgical oncology, sharing experiences, and inspiring one another to reach new heights.

The leadership of IASO and organizing committee of NATCON 24 have worked in close collaboration to curate a scientific feast catering to all sections of IASO including trainees and practicing surgeons.

I sincerely extend my best wishes for the grand success of NATCON 24


Prof. SVS Deo

President
Indian Association of Surgical Oncology (IASO)

WELCOME TO IASO NATCON 2024

Dear Members,

Greetings from NATCON 2024, Bengaluru!

It is with immense pleasure that we extend our invitation to you for the 37th National Conference of the Indian Association of Surgical Oncology, “NATCON 2024”. The event is scheduled to take place from September 26 to 29, 2024, at Clark’s Convention Centre, Off Airport Road, Bengaluru, INDIA.


The theme of the conference is “Emerge Evolve Excel.”

Our objective for this conference is to engage with distinguished oncologists, exchange knowledge, stay abreast of the latest innovations and changing trends, and provide guidance to emerging young oncologists based on your vast experience. The success of this conference hinges on the active participation of our esteemed members in large numbers.

Bengaluru is easily accessible by air, rail, and road. The venue, Clark’s Convention Centre, is conveniently located near the International Airport. The pleasant weather and warm hospitality of the garden city are sure to captivate you.

We urge you to join us in large numbers in Namma Bengaluru. We guarantee an Academic Feast, a Gastronomic Treat, and a Cultural Extravaganza.

We eagerly await your confirmation of participation and kindly request your gracious presence.

Accommodations are open at the venue and nearby hotel. Please book at the earliest.

Looking forward to your enthusiastic response.

Best Regards,
IASO Office
